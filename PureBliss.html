FARM STRUCTURES AND BUILDINGS

DESCRIPTION OF FARM STRUCTURES AND FARM BUILDINGS

Generally, in most commercial farms as well as other organized small scale farms, farm structures and building are used for storing their farm produce, housing their farm animals, keeping farm machineries and equipment; and to carry out other activities on the farm.These structures and buildings may be temporary or permanent which may be built of permanent materials like concrete, wood, aluminum/asbestos sheets, iron or metal sheets, etc. or built of temporary materials like mud, grasses bamboo, palm fronds, etc
